🏁 MX StopWatch – Precision Timing & Performance Analytics

MX StopWatch is a feature-rich stopwatch app designed for motorsport enthusiasts, athletes, and fitness trainers who need accurate timing and detailed analytics. Whether you're on the track, in the gym, or timing your laps, MX StopWatch helps you track, analyze, and improve your performance.

🔥 Key Features

⏱️ Precision Timing

  • Start/Stop Button – Single tap for instant timing.
  • Lap Timing – Auto-detect fastest and slowest laps.
  • Sector Timing – Analyze performance in different segments.
  • Total Time Display – Track elapsed time accurately.

📊 Detailed Analytics

  • Session Analytics – View fastest lap, slowest lap, and consistency scores.
  • History – Review past performances.
  • Compare Sessions – Side-by-side performance insights.
  • Consistency Metrics – Percentage-based performance scoring.

📍 GPS Integration

  • GPS Tracking – Auto-record lap and sector times based on location.
  • Map View – Place markers for lap start and sectors.
  • Location-Based Triggers – Automatic lap timing.

⚙️ Customizable Sessions

  • Session Naming – Personalize session names.
  • Edit & Delete – Modify or remove session details.

🎨 User-Friendly Interface

  • Intuitive Design – Clean and easy-to-use UI.
  • Dark/Light Mode – Toggle themes for better visibility.
  • Vibration Feedback – Haptic feedback for button presses.

📤 Data Export & Sharing

  • Web Integration – Upload sessions for analysis.
  • Delete from Web – Remove uploaded sessions.

🔧 Advanced Features

  • Wake Lock – Keeps the screen on during sessions.
  • Vibration Alerts – Alerts for lap/sector completions.
  • Customizable Username – Personalize your experience.

🎯 Use Cases

  • 🏎️ Motorsport – Track lap/sector times.
  • 🏃 Athletes – Interval training.
  • 💪 Fitness Enthusiasts – Workout timing.
  • 📈 Coaches & Trainers – Session analysis.
