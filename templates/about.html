About the App

MX StopWatch is a professional-grade stopwatch application designed specifically for motorsports enthusiasts, racers, and teams who demand precision timing and comprehensive performance analytics.

Originally developed as "Stoperica," the app has evolved into MX StopWatch with enhanced features tailored for motorsports applications, including motocross, karting, and circuit racing.

With features like automatic lap triggering via GPS, live session sharing, and detailed data analysis, MX StopWatch provides everything you need to measure and improve your track performance.

Key Features

Precision Timing

Millisecond-accurate stopwatch with lap and sector timing capabilities, perfect for analyzing every segment of your race.

  • Lap timing
  • Sector timing
  • Best/worst lap tracking

GPS Auto-Triggers

Set markers on a map to automatically trigger laps and sectors when passing specific locations on the track.

  • Start/finish line detection
  • Sector point marking
  • Course mapping

Live Team Sessions

Host or join live timing sessions with real-time synchronization across multiple devices for team coordination.

  • Real-time updates
  • Multiple participants
  • Host/client architecture

Advanced Analytics

Detailed analytics dashboard to review your performance and identify areas for improvement.

  • Lap time consistency
  • Sector analysis
  • Performance trends

Session Comparison

Compare multiple sessions side-by-side to track your progress or compare with teammates.

  • Side-by-side comparison
  • Lap delta analysis
  • Performance benchmarks

Comprehensive History

Access your complete session history with all recorded data for long-term performance tracking.

  • Full session archive
  • Detailed lap records
  • Export capabilities
